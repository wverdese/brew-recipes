# 🍺 Alcoholic Ginger Beer w/ Ginger Bug (5L, 4–5% ABV)

## Target

* Batch Size: 5 L
* OG: ~1.070
* FG: ~1.008–1.012
* ABV: ~4.8–5.2%
* Carbonation: High
* Sweetness: Added post-fermentation

---

# 🧫 Ginger Bug (Start 4–6 Days Before Brew Day)

## Goal

* Start: 250 ml
* Use: 150–200 ml (strained)

### Day 1

* 250 ml water (20–25°C)
* 15 g grated ginger
* 15 g rørsukker

### Day 2

* 8 g ginger
* 8 g rørsukker

### Day 3

* 8 g ginger
* 8 g rørsukker

### Day 4

* 8 g ginger
* 8 g rørsukker

### Day 5

* 5 g ginger
* 5 g rørsukker

### Brew Day Prep (same day, morning)

* 5–8 g ginger
* 10 g rørsukker

→ Wait 4–8 hours before pitching

---

# 🍶 Ginger Tincture (Flexible Timing)

## Ingredients

* 75 g fresh ginger (finely grated)
* 200 ml vodka (40%)

## Process

1. Add ginger to jar
2. Cover with vodka
3. Shake well

## Extraction Time (IMPORTANT)

* Minimum: **24 hours (usable)**
* Recommended: **2–5 days (ideal balance)**
* Extended: **up to ~7–10 days (stronger, more intense)**

👉 You can start this **anytime before fermentation finishes**
👉 It only needs to be ready by **Day 7–10 (post-fermentation stage)**

## Before use

* Strain through cheesecloth or coffee filter
* Keep only the liquid (tincture)

---

# 🛒 Ingredients (Brew Day)

* Rørsukker: 400 g
* Brun farin: 600 g
* Fresh ginger: 300 g
* Lime: 1 (zest + juice)
* Salt: 0.5–1 g
* Water: ~6 L
* Ginger bug: 150–200 ml

### Post-fermentation

* Ginger tincture (prepared above)
* SodaStream Lemonade No Sugar syrup

---

# 🔥 Brew Day

## 1. Boil

Add:

* 5.5–6 L water
* 200 g ginger
* 400 g rørsukker
* 600 g brun farin

Simmer **30 minutes**

---

## 2. Flameout / Steep

Add:

* 100 g ginger
* Lime zest

Steep **20 minutes**

---

## 3. Cooling

Cool to **20–22°C**

Add:

* Lime juice
* Salt

---

## 4. Fermentation

* Transfer to fermenter
* Add **150–200 ml active ginger bug**
* Ferment at **20–22°C**

---

# ⏱️ Fermentation Timeline

| Day  | Stage               |
| ---- | ------------------- |
| 0    | Pitch               |
| 1–2  | Lag/start           |
| 2–4  | Active fermentation |
| 5–7  | Slowing down        |
| 7–10 | Final attenuation   |

---

# ✅ Ferment to Completion (CRITICAL)

Do NOT bottle early.

You want:

* No bubbling
* Gravity stable for 2 days

Target FG: **~1.008–1.012**

---

# 🌶️ Post-Fermentation Ginger Boost (Tincture)

## When:

👉 After fermentation is complete (Day 7–10)

## How:

1. Add **25 ml tincture**
2. Stir gently
3. Taste

Then adjust:

* 25–40 ml → balanced
* 40–60 ml → strong
* 60+ ml → aggressive 🔥

---

# 🍾 Bottling

Add priming sugar:

* **5–6 g/L → 25–30 g total**

Bottle in strong bottles.

---

# 🫧 Carbonation

* 2–4 days at 20–22°C
* Then refrigerate

---

# 🍋 Back-Sweetening (Serving Stage)

## Per glass (recommended)

* 5–15 ml SodaStream Lemonade syrup per 250 ml
* Stir gently

---

## Flavor Effect

* Adds sweetness + acidity
* Brightens ginger
* Balances spice

---

# 🧊 Conditioning & Storage

* Drink after ~1 week
* Best within 3–4 weeks
* Store cold after carbonation

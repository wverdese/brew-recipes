# 🍺 Aurora – Nordic Italian Pilsner

## Target Stats

* **OG**: ~1.050
* **FG**: ~1.009
* **ABV**: ~5.3%
* **IBU**: ~22
* **SRM/EBC**: ~4 / 7–8 EBC

---

## Grain Bill (Braumeister-friendly)

| Grain            |  Amount |   % |
| ---------------- | ------: | --: |
| BEST Pilsen Malt | 4.50 kg | 90% |
| BEST Vienna Malt | 0.50 kg | 10% |

---

## Water

### Profile (recommended)

* **Ca**: 50–60 ppm
* **Mg**: 5–10 ppm
* **Cl**: 40–50 ppm
* **SO₄**: 60–80 ppm

### pH

* 5.3 to 5.4

---

## Recipe

### Mash Schedule

**Fill Braumeister with ~25–26L of water**.

| Minute | Step              | Target Temp (°C) | Action / Notes             |
| ------ | ----------------- | ---------------: | -------------------------- |
| 0      | Heat Strike Water |              ~52 | Heat water before dough-in |
| 1      | Dough-in          |               52 | Add grains, stir gently    |
| 1–10   | Ramp Up           |          52 → 64 | Controlled rise            |
| 10–60  | Saccharification  |               64 | Builds fermentability      |
| 60–75  | Malt Balance Rest |               68 | Adds gentle body           |
| 75–85  | Mash-out ramp     |          68 → 75 | Raise to mash-out          |
| 85–95  | Mash-out rest     |               75 | Improve lautering          |
| 95     | End Mash          |                - | Remove malt pipe           |

### Lauter / Sparge

* Remove malt pipe after mash-out.
* Sparge with **75°C** water.
* Collect **~26 L** pre-boil.
* Expected pre-boil gravity: **~1.044**.

### Hop Schedule (~22 IBU total)

| Minute | Stage | Temperature | Hop Addition              | Notes                  |
| -----: | ----- | ----------- | ------------------------- | ---------------------- |
|     60 | Boil  | 100°C       | 25 g Hallertau Mittelfrüh | Main bitterness        |
|     15 | Boil  | 100°C       | 15 g Hallertau Mittelfrüh | Light noble hop flavor |

### Cooling

* Chill to **15°C**.
* Transfer to fermenter.
* Oxygenate well before pitching.

### Fermentation

* **Yeast:** Lallemand Novalager, 1 sachet.
* **Pitch temperature:** 15°C
* **Primary fermentation:** 15–16°C
* **Cleanup:** raise to 18°C for 2 days near final gravity
* **Cold crash:** 2–4°C for 3–5 days

### Fermentation Timeline

| Day   | Stage               |    Temp | SG (approx.) | Notes                    |
| ----- | ------------------- | ------: | -----------: | ------------------------ |
| 0     | Pitch yeast         |    15°C |        1.050 | Aerate well              |
| 1–2   | Active fermentation | 15–16°C |  1.035–1.040 | Steady activity          |
| 3–5   | Main attenuation    | 15–16°C |  1.015–1.020 | Keep temperature stable  |
| 6–7   | Near FG             |    16°C |  1.010–1.012 | Raise to 18°C            |
| 8–9   | Cleanup             |    18°C |        1.009 | Hold warm                |
| 10    | Cold crash          |   2–4°C |       Stable | Begin clarification      |
| 10–17 | Lagering            |   2–4°C |       Stable | Optional but recommended |

### Dry-Hopping Schedule

| Day | Expected SG | Stage      | Temperature | Hop Addition | Notes                                        |
| --- | ----------: | ---------- | ----------: | ------------ | -------------------------------------------- |
| -   |           - | No dry hop |           - | -            | Intentionally restrained and budget-friendly |

### Packaging

* Aim for **2.5–2.6 vol CO₂**.
* Serve cold at **4–6°C**.

### 🧊 Conditioning Time – Overview

| Method      | Conditioning Time | Notes                                       |
| ----------- | ----------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| **Kegged**  | 1–2 weeks         | Best after a short cold conditioning period |
| **Bottled** | 2–3 weeks         | Carbonate warm, then chill before serving   |

---

## Final Notes

✅ A continental malt profile that keeps the beer pale and drinkable.
✅ The 15-minute Hallertau addition gives subtle floral/herbal character without making it hop-forward.
✅ Novalager keeps fermentation practical and clean.

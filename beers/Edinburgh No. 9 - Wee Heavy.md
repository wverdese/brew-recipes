## Recipe

### Mash Schedule

**Fill Braumeister with ~19.0 L of mash water** (thick mash for Wee Heavy body).

| Minute | Step                       | Temp (¬∞C) | Action / Notes                                                                                                                     |
| ------ | -------------------------- | --------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 0      | Dough-in                   | 68        | Add all grains to malt pipe; stir well. Thick mash (~2.5 L/kg) is intentional for body.                                            |
| 0‚Äì60   | Saccharification           | 68        | Hold 60 minutes. Circulation may be slower with a full malt pipe.                                                                  |
| 60‚Äì70  | Mash-out                   | 75        | Raise to 75¬∞C, hold 10 minutes.                                                                                                    |
| 70     | Lift malt pipe             | ‚Äî         | Allow the first runnings to drain fully into the kettle.                                                                           |

#### Mash Notes

* Stir well at dough-in to prevent dough balls.
* Thick mash improves mouthfeel and sweetness ‚Äî ideal for Wee Heavy.
* Caramelization pot will reduce while you proceed with sparging and heating to boil.

### Lauter / Sparge

* Sparge with **6.5 L of 75¬∞C water** over the malt pipe.
* Aim for **~26 L pre-boil** volume in the main kettle.
* Expected pre-boil gravity: **1.060‚Äì1.065**.
* Stop sparging early if volume approaches 26 L ‚Äî concentrated wort is desirable.

---

### Hop & Boil Schedule (90 minutes)

**Start the main kettle boil once you reach ~26 L.
Meanwhile keep the first-runnings pot boiling HARD.**

> **Goal for syrup reduction:** reduce 2‚Äì3 L down to **~300‚Äì500 mL** of thick, deep-brown syrup that coats a spoon.
> Time required: **20‚Äì40 minutes** depending on heat.

| Minute | Stage                     | Temp  | Addition                   | Amount      | Notes                                                    |
| ------ | ------------------------- | ----- | -------------------------- | ----------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| 90     | Start of Boil             | 100¬∞C | ‚Äî                          | ‚Äî           | Begin 90 min boil. Syrup reduction continues separately. |

#### Notes on Caramelization

* When finished, the syrup will be:

  * noticeably darker
  * thick like molasses
  * aromatic with toffee/burnt sugar
  * reduced to roughly **15‚Äì20%** of its original volume

* Add slowly while stirring to avoid sinking or scorching.
